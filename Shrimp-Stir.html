Description

This shrimp stir fry recipe with frozen vegetables, frozen shrimp, and a simple stir fry sauce â€” comes together in less than 30 minutes for a quick and easy dinner! Serve over rice or with noodles, and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix chicken stock, soy sauce, cornstarch, and garlic in a bowl; season with salt and pepper
  2. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until oil shimmers; cook and stir vegetables in hot oil until softened, about 4 minutes.
  3. Add shrimp; cook and stir until shrimp begin to turn pink, about 3 minutes.
  4. Stir chicken stock mixture into vegetable-shrimp mixture
  5. Continue to cook and stir until vegetables and shrimp are coated and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
